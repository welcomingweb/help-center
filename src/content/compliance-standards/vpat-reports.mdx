# 📑 VPAT® & Compliance Reports

A Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT®) documents how your products or services comply with accessibility standards. Welcoming Web provides expert VPAT® documentation tailored to your business needs, ensuring compliance with international accessibility regulations such as WCAG, ADA, and Section 508.

## Why VPAT® is Important

VPAT® reports help your organization:

- **Meet Procurement Requirements:** Essential for government and enterprise contracts.
- **Demonstrate Compliance:** Clearly communicate your accessibility commitment.
- **Enhance Market Competitiveness:** Stand out by showcasing accessibility readiness.
