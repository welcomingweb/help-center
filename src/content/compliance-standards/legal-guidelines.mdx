## Compliance Responsibilities

As an organization, you have the legal responsibility to:

- Ensure your digital services are accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities.
- Regularly audit and update your digital content and platforms to meet ongoing accessibility standards.
- Provide transparent documentation of compliance efforts, such as VPAT® reports.

## Risk of Non-Compliance

Ignoring or inadequately addressing accessibility standards can lead to:

- Legal penalties, fines, and litigation.
- Damaged brand reputation and loss of consumer trust.
- Missed opportunities in regulated sectors, especially government and education.
