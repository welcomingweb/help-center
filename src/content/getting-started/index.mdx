# Welcome to WelcomingWeb

Let's get your accessibility journey started! Follow these simple steps to quickly set up your account, add your first website, and interpret your accessibility metrics and scans.

## Creating Your Account

Quickly set up your free WelcomingWeb account:

<Steps>

### Step 1: Register Your Account

Visit the [WelcomingWeb Registration Page](https://app.welcomingweb.com/register) and enter your basic details to get started.

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a confirmation email. Click on the provided link to activate your WelcomingWeb account.

### Step 3: Access Your Dashboard

After verification, [log in to your dashboard](https://app.welcomingweb.com/login) to begin enhancing your site's accessibility.

</Steps>

## Adding Your First Website

Easily add your site and start your accessibility journey:

<Steps>

### Step 1: Go to "Add Site"

From your dashboard, click on **"Add Site"** and enter your website URL and desired site name.

### Step 2: Confirm Your Site

Review the website details carefully, ensuring accuracy, then click **"Confirm"**.

### Step 3: Initial Scan

WelcomingWeb will immediately perform an initial accessibility scan, providing valuable insights and actionable metrics.

</Steps>

## Interpreting Accessibility Scan Results

Our comprehensive scans provide clear metrics to understand your site's accessibility:

- **Accessibility Score** : Indicates how easily people with disabilities can access your site.
- **Performance Score** : Evaluates your site's speed and responsiveness.
- **SEO Score** : Measures optimization for search engine visibility.
- **Best Practices Score** : Reviews adherence to modern web standards.

<Callout type="info">
Detailed results and recommendations are available in your site's analytics dashboard.
</Callout>
