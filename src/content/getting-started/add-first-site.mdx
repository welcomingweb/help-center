# Add Your First Site

Follow these easy steps to add your first site and begin improving its accessibility.

<Steps>

### Open the Dashboard

Log into your [WelcomingWeb Dashboard](https://app.welcomingweb.com/dashboard). Upon your first login, you'll see a welcome screen.

<HotspotImage
  src="/screenshot/accessibility-dashboard.png"
  hotspots={[
    { x: 50, y: 35, title: 'Create First Site', description: 'Click this button to start adding your first site.' },
  ]}
/>

### Select Your Widget

Choose between the Free Widget or the recommended PRO-Accessibility Widget based on your accessibility needs.

- **Free Widget**: Provides basic accessibility features.
- **PRO Widget**: Offers full compliance and comprehensive accessibility features.

<HotspotImage
  src="/screenshot/add-site-select-widget.png"
  hotspots={[
    { x: 30, y: 45, title: 'Free Widget', description: 'Basic features, ideal for personal or smaller projects.' },
    { x: 70, y: 45, title: 'PRO Widget', description: 'Advanced accessibility features, compliance, and real-time monitoring.' },
  ]}
/>

### Enter Site Information

Fill in the required fields:

- **Service Name**: Give a clear and recognizable name.
- **Website URL**: Provide the full URL of the site you want to add.

Click **Next** to proceed.

### Confirm and Start Scanning

Review your details, and once confirmed, WelcomingWeb will immediately begin crawling and analyzing your website for accessibility improvements.

</Steps>

You're now ready to [configure your widget](/getting-started/configure-widget).
