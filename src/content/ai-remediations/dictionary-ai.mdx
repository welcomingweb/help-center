# AI Dictionary

<Callout type="info">
Welcomingweb's AI Dictionary feature delivers instant definitions and explanations directly within your website content, greatly enhancing readability and comprehension.
</Callout>

## How AI Dictionary Works

The AI Dictionary feature intelligently detects complex or technical terms on your website and provides immediate, context-aware definitions:

- **Contextual Understanding:** Automatically identifies key words needing clarification.
- **Dynamic Definitions:** Provides precise, real-time explanations in the user's preferred language.
- **Integrated Learning:** Definitions appear directly within your content for seamless learning.

## Activating AI Dictionary

Enable the AI Dictionary in just a few simple steps:

<Steps>

### Step 1: Navigate to Your Dashboard

- Log into your WelcomingWeb admin panel.
- Visit your site's accessibility settings.

### Step 2: Enable Dictionary Feature

- Toggle on the "AI Dictionary" feature.
- Click **Save** to activate.

### Step 3: Test and Confirm

- Open your website and hover over or select terms identified by the AI Dictionary to verify definitions appear as intended.

</Steps>

## Customizing Dictionary Settings

You can personalize the AI Dictionary behavior:

- **Definition Presentation:** Choose between tooltip, popup, or inline definitions.
- **Term Sensitivity:** Adjust how aggressively the dictionary identifies terms.

```html
<script
  src="https://cdn-01.welcomingweb.com/a11y-widget.bundle.js"
  data-widget-id="YOUR_WIDGET_ID"
  data-ai-dictionary-enabled="true"
  data-dictionary-display-mode="tooltip">
</script>
```
