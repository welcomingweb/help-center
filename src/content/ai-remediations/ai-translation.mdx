# AI Translation

<Callout type="info">
WelcomingWeb's AI Translation feature automatically translates your website's content into over 100 languages, enabling you to reach and engage global audiences effortlessly.
</Callout>

## How AI Translation Works

AI Translation dynamically translates your site's content, ensuring it's easily understandable by users worldwide:

- **Real-Time Translation:** Instantly translates webpage content.
- **Contextual Accuracy:** Utilizes advanced AI to ensure translations are accurate and contextually relevant.
- **Automatic Detection:** Automatically recognizes the user's browser language and translates accordingly.

## Activating AI Translation

Follow these straightforward steps to enable translation:

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open Your Dashboard

- Log in to your WelcomingWeb dashboard.
- Navigate to your site's accessibility settings.

### Step 2: Enable Translation

- Activate the "AI Translation" feature.
- Select the languages you wish to support.
- Save your configuration.

### Step 3: Verify Translations

- Visit your site and confirm translations are accurate and accessible.

</Steps>

## Customizing Translation Settings

Further customize your translation options:

- **Language Selector:** Enable a manual language selection dropdown for visitors.
- **Default Language:** Specify the default language displayed to visitors.

```html
<script
  src="https://cdn-01.welcomingweb.com/a11y-widget.bundle.js"
  data-widget-id="YOUR_WIDGET_ID"
  data-ai-translation-enabled="true"
  data-default-language="en">
</script>
```
