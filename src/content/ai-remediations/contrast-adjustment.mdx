# Contrast Adjustment

<Callout type="info">
WelcomingWeb automatically optimizes contrast on your website, making your content easily readable for all visitors, including those with visual impairments.
</Callout>

## How AI-Powered Contrast Adjustment Works

WelcomingWeb's contrast adjustment tool dynamically analyzes your website’s content and adjusts colors to ensure optimal readability and accessibility:

- **Dynamic Adjustments:** Automatically analyzes text and background colors for insufficient contrast.
- **Adaptive Colors:** Uses intelligent algorithms to adjust colors without compromising your site's branding.
- **Real-Time Optimization:** Instantly adapts as your site's content changes.

## Enabling Contrast Adjustment

To activate the AI-powered contrast adjustment feature, follow these simple steps:

<Steps>

### Step 1: Log in to Your WelcomingWeb Dashboard

- Navigate to your site’s accessibility settings.

### Step 2: Select the Contrast Adjustment Feature

- Enable the "Contrast Adjustment" toggle.
- Save your changes.

### Step 3: Test and Verify

- Visit your website and confirm that the contrast levels have been optimized.

</Steps>

## Customizing Contrast Settings

Advanced users can fine-tune settings:

- **Contrast Modes:** Choose between "dark", "light", or "inverted" contrast modes.
- **Color Thresholds:** Adjust minimum contrast ratio thresholds.

```html
<script
  src="https://cdn-01.welcomingweb.com/a11y-widget.bundle.js"
  data-widget-id="YOUR_WIDGET_ID"
  data-theme="auto"
  data-button-color-mode="SOLID"
  data-enable-contrast-adjustment="true">
</script>
```

## Benefits of Using AI Contrast Adjustment

- **Improved Readability:** Ensures content clarity for users with low vision or color blindness.
- **Compliance:** Helps meet accessibility standards such as WCAG 2.1 guidelines.
- **User Engagement:** Reduces bounce rates and improves user experience.
