# AI Image Descriptions

<Callout type="info">
Welcomingweb's AI-driven image description tool automatically analyzes images on your site and generates descriptive alt texts, enhancing accessibility for visually impaired users.
</Callout>

## How AI Image Descriptions Work

The AI-powered image description feature ensures your images are accessible by providing descriptive alt text automatically:

- **Intelligent Analysis:** Analyzes images without existing alt descriptions.
- **Automated Descriptions:** Generates detailed and contextually accurate descriptions.
- **Real-Time Processing:** Automatically processes new images as they're added to your site.

## Activating AI Image Descriptions

Follow these straightforward steps to enable AI-generated image descriptions:

<Steps>

### Step 1: Log in to Your Dashboard

- Access your WelcomingWeb dashboard.
- Go to your site's accessibility settings.

### Step 2: Enable Image Descriptions

- Turn on the "AI Image Descriptions" toggle.
- Click **Save** to apply.

### Step 3: Verify Image Descriptions

- Visit your website to confirm images without original alt texts now have AI-generated descriptions.

</Steps>

## Customizing Image Description Settings

Advanced users can manage how descriptions are generated:

- **Language Preference:** Set your preferred language for descriptions.
- **Quality Control:** Adjust sensitivity for the detail and accuracy of generated descriptions.

```html
<script
  src="https://cdn-01.welcomingweb.com/a11y-widget.bundle.js"
  data-widget-id="YOUR_WIDGET_ID"
  data-image-descriptions-enabled="true"
  data-language="en">
</script>
```

## Benefits of AI Image Descriptions

- **Enhanced Accessibility:** Makes visual content accessible to screen reader users.
- **Improved SEO:** Descriptive alt texts can improve search engine rankings.
- **Compliance:** Supports adherence to accessibility guidelines (WCAG 2.1).

## Troubleshooting Tips

<Callout type="warning">
Common checks if image descriptions fail to generate:
</Callout>

- Confirm the widget script is correctly installed.
- Ensure images don't already have alt texts (AI descriptions only apply to missing alt texts).
- Check browser console for script errors or conflicts.
