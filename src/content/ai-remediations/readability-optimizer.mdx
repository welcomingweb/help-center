# AI Readability Optimizer

<Callout type="info">
WelcomingWeb's AI Readability Optimizer intelligently improves your site's text clarity and comprehension, making content accessible to a broader audience.
</Callout>

## How AI Readability Optimization Works

The AI Readability Optimizer scans your content in real-time and automatically adjusts it for maximum readability:

- **Intelligent Text Analysis:** AI evaluates sentence complexity, vocabulary, and readability levels.
- **Dynamic Content Adjustment:** Suggests simplified phrasing, clearer wording, and improved sentence structures.
- **Real-Time Optimization:** Continually optimizes newly added or updated content.

## Customizing Readability Settings

Advanced customization includes:

- **Readability Levels:** Select from predefined readability targets (basic, intermediate, advanced).
- **Language Settings:** Optimize readability specifically tailored to your target audience language.

```html
<script
  src="https://cdn-01.welcomingweb.com/a11y-widget.bundle.js"
  data-widget-id="YOUR_WIDGET_ID"
  data-ai-readability-optimizer-enabled="true"
  data-readability-level="intermediate">
</script>
```
